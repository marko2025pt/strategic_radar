## Core Hardware Products

PARTTEAM & OEMKIOSKS manufactures 100+ standard hardware models across
kiosk, digital billboard, and interactive display categories, all designed
and produced in Portugal. Custom form factors and OEM/ODM variants are
available across the entire product line.

- **Multimedia Kiosks** — indoor and outdoor self-service kiosks for information, wayfinding, and interaction
- **Transactional & Payment Kiosks** — kiosks with integrated payment terminals (Adyen, PAX, Valina, Ingenico) for ticketing, bill payment, and retail transactions
- **QSR Self-Order Kiosks** — self-ordering and self-checkout kiosks for quick service restaurants (branded line: NOMYU QSR)
- **Self-Checkout Kiosks** — retail self-checkout with barcode scanning and product scale integration (branded: SELFCASHY, YSCO Market)
- **Digital Billboards / MUPIs** — indoor and outdoor digital signage totems and wall-mounted displays (branded lines: NOMYU, AVENUXYS, CITIO, CUPERTINO, ZYTEC)
- **Passenger Information Displays** — real-time passenger information (RTPI) screens for transport hubs and bus stops
- **Interactive Tables** — multitouch interactive tables for retail, hospitality, and public spaces; supports collaborative and customer-facing use cases
- **Queue Management Kiosks** — ticket dispensing and queue management systems (branded: QTICKET TOUCH)
- **TVM — Ticket Vending Machines** — outdoor multi-touch ticketing machines for transport (branded: TRANSACT TVM)
- **Banking Kiosks** — self-service terminals for banking and financial services; deployed in Portugal, Qatar, and other markets
- **Hotel Self-Service Kiosks** — check-in/check-out kiosks for hospitality with PMS integration (branded: ONGON)

## Core Software and Services

PARTTEAM & OEMKIOSKS develops proprietary software products that are sold
bundled with hardware or as standalone licences. All software is developed
in-house and designed to integrate with third-party payment, POS, and
data systems.

### Software

- **YSnack / YCanteen** — self-ordering software for QSR and food service; supports menu management, upsell logic, and POS integration
- **YSCO Market** — self-checkout software for retail; supports barcode scanning, product scale recognition, and multiple payment methods
- **YPortal** — information and interaction portal software for education, public services, and corporate environments; available as mobile app
- **The Brain** — cloud-based remote management and monitoring platform for all deployed hardware; enables real-time diagnostics, content updates, and uptime tracking across distributed kiosk networks
- **Custom CMS** — content management for digital signage and DOOH networks; supports programmatic content scheduling and campaign management

### Services

- **OEM/ODM Manufacturing** — custom hardware design and production for partners and distributors who resell under their own brand
- **Custom Development** — bespoke kiosk and software solutions for specific client requirements
- **Remote Monitoring & Support** — via The Brain platform
- **Field Installation & Maintenance** — on-site deployment and after-sales technical support
- **Renting & Leasing** — hardware rental and renting programmes in partnership with Grenke financing

## Revenue Model and Sales Motion

### Revenue Streams

- **Hardware sales** — one-time sale of kiosk and digital billboard units, standard and custom
- **Software licences** — YSnack, YPortal, YSCO Market, and custom software sold as standalone licences or bundled with hardware
- **Maintenance and support contracts** — annual service agreements, remote monitoring via The Brain, and on-site technical support
- **OEM/ODM manufacturing contracts** — custom hardware production for partners and distributors who resell under their own brand
- **Renting and leasing** — hardware rental programmes for clients who prefer OpEx over CapEx (in partnership with Grenke financing)

### Sales Motion

- **Direct enterprise sales** — to large retail chains, restaurant groups, hotel operators, and corporate clients
- **Public tender (procurement)** — primary channel for government, municipal, transport, and healthcare contracts; EU and national tender processes
- **Partner and distributor network** — indirect sales through certified resellers and integrators across international markets
- **Inbound via configurator** — online custom solution configurator drives lead generation from the website

### Typical Deal Profile

- **Public sector contracts:** longer sales cycles (6–18 months), higher contract values, repeat deployments across municipalities or transport networks
- **Enterprise / QSR / retail:** medium sales cycles (3–9 months), driven by rollout programmes across multiple locations
- **OEM/ODM:** project-based, high-volume, longer-term manufacturing agreements with partners
