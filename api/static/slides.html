04 / 09
Knowledge Base
by Marco Martins — an Ironhack project
Not a generic industry overview — the client's strategic identity, encoded and queryable.
business_profile.md
Company identity, products, verticals and revenue model. Gives the system a grounded understanding of the business — who the client is, what they sell, and which markets they serve.
strategic_direction.md
Active strategic objectives with explicit signal relevance criteria. The core filter — every incoming signal is measured against these objectives to determine if it warrants attention.
competitor_registry.json
Controlled list of monitored competitors with structured metadata. Keeps research focused on the right players and prevents noise from irrelevant market actors.
technology_watchlist.md
Emerging technologies relevant to the client's ecosystem. Keeps the system aware of disruptive trends before they become mainstream — enabling early positioning.
